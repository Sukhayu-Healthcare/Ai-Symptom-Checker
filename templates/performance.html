05

Evaluation Methodology

How each layer of the pipeline was scored independently.

🔎

Retrieval Evaluation

Each patient case is embedded and matched against the Qdrant vector index using a weighted k-NN search. The predicted disease and triage zone are compared against ground truth across the full held-out sample.

🧠

LLM Evaluation

Retrieved cases are passed to the Groq-hosted LLM, which reasons over context, may ask clarifying follow-ups, and outputs a final disease and zone. Its output is scored end-to-end against ground truth on a smaller, human-reviewable sample.

📐

Metrics Used

Disease and zone accuracy, precision, recall and F1 score are computed per-class and macro-averaged. Response time is measured end-to-end, from query to final structured output.
